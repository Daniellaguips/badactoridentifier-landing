# Privacy Policy Addendum for Community Leaders
**Effective Date:** [Insert Date]
**Version:** 2.0

This Privacy Policy Addendum ("Addendum") supplements the [Platform Name] Privacy Policy (the "Policy") and applies to all users designated as Community Leaders ("Leader," "you," or "your").

---

## 1. Role of the Parties
- The Company is the data controller for Platform operations (accounts, logs, system security).
- Leaders act as independent data controllers for any personal data they upload, submit, or process about their community members.
- Leaders are solely responsible for ensuring that such uploads are lawful, authorized, and consistent with applicable privacy and data-protection laws.

---

## 2. Data You May Process as Leader
- Information about your community's members or applicants, such as:
  - Name, date of birth, and city of residence;
  - Contact identifiers (email/phone, usually in hashed form);
  - Employment or role information;
  - Photographs or voluntarily provided identifiers;
  - Reports submitted regarding misconduct or safety concerns.

---

## 3. Data Minimization and Restrictions
- You must limit uploads to necessary and proportionate information for safety and vetting purposes.
- You must not upload sensitive categories (financial, medical, biometric) unless expressly required and permitted by law.
- You may not attempt to re-identify anonymized or hashed data.
- You may not retain or repurpose Platform data outside authorized community use.

---

## 4. Confidentiality Obligations
- All data and reports accessed through the Platform are confidential.
- You may not disclose such data externally, except:
  - Within your approved community for legitimate vetting, or
  - As required by law, subject to advance notice to the Company where legally permissible.

---

## 5. Data Security
- You must implement appropriate technical and organizational safeguards to protect data you upload or access.
- You must immediately notify the Company of any suspected or actual data breach.
- You must cooperate with the Company in investigating and mitigating security incidents.

---

## 6. Legal Basis and Transparency
- You represent and warrant that:
  - You have a valid legal basis (e.g., consent, legitimate interests) for uploading or processing member data.
  - You have provided members with clear notice of such processing, including the Member Notice required by the Platform.

---

## 7. Member Rights
- Members may request access, correction, or deletion of their information.
- You must promptly forward any member rights requests to the Company.
- You agree to cooperate with the Company in resolving such requests in compliance with law.

---

## 8. Company Use of Uploaded Data
- By uploading data, you grant the Company a broad license (per Section 6.1 of the TOS) to:
  - Use the data to operate the Platform,
  - Improve and develop Platform features, and
  - Create aggregated, anonymized, or de-identified data products.
- The Company will not resell or commercially exploit raw identifiable data outside these purposes.

---

## 9. Cross-Border Processing
- Platform data may be stored or processed in jurisdictions outside the user's country.
- You are responsible for ensuring that uploads comply with cross-border transfer laws where applicable.

---

## 10. Liability and Indemnification
- You acknowledge that unlawful or unauthorized uploads may expose you and your community to regulatory penalties, damages, or litigation.
- You agree to indemnify and hold harmless the Company for any claims, fines, or damages arising from your data processing activities.

---

## 11. Survival
- These obligations survive termination of your Leader status and remain binding for as long as you retain or process data obtained through the Platform.

---

**By serving as a Community Leader, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to comply with this Privacy Policy Addendum and all applicable privacy laws and regulations.**
