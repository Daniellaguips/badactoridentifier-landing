# Privacy Policy for Members
**Effective Date:** [Insert Date]
**Version:** 1.0

This Privacy Policy ("Policy") explains how [App Operator Legal Entity] ("Company," "we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and protects information about Members ("you") on [Platform Name].

---

## 1. Information We Collect
- Profile information you provide (name, photo, bio, community affiliations, city/general location).
- Private identifiers (email, phone, full date of birth, street address, government ID numbers) — stored securely and never made public.
- Reports or reviews you submit.
- Technical data (IP address, device info, activity logs).

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information
- To operate your account and profile.
- To enable safety vetting and community protections.
- To notify you about account-related activity.
- To improve and develop Platform features.
- To generate **aggregated and anonymized insights** shared with other Members and Communities.
- To comply with legal obligations and respond to law enforcement requests.

---

## 3. Reports and Visibility
- Reports are user-generated and may reference you.
- Your profile (excluding private identifiers) may be made visible to other Members or Communities.
- Reports and safety notations may be linked to your profile.
- You consent to this as a condition of membership.

---

## 4. Data Sharing
- We do not sell your identifiable data.
- We may share data:
  - With Community Leaders for legitimate safety purposes,
  - With law enforcement when legally required or in our discretion for safety,
  - With service providers (e.g., hosting, security, notifications).

---

## 5. Data Retention
- We retain data as long as necessary for safety, audit, and legal purposes.
- You may delete your profile, but reports may be retained to protect other Members.

---

## 6. Your Rights
- You may access, correct, or delete your profile information.
- You may request a copy of your data via [Support Email].
- We may decline deletion requests for reports linked to safety purposes.

---

## 7. Security
- Sensitive identifiers are hashed or encrypted where possible.
- We maintain technical and organizational safeguards.
- No system is 100% secure; you use the Platform at your own risk.

---

## 8. International Transfers
- Data may be processed outside your country of residence.
- We implement safeguards for cross-border transfers where required.

---

## 9. Updates
- We may update this Policy at any time.
- Continued use of the Platform after changes constitutes acceptance.
