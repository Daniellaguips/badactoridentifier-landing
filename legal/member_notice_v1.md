## Your Privacy Rights

### What Information We Collect
- **Identity Information**: Your name and contact details (stored in encrypted form)
- **Community Role**: Your position and participation in our community
- **Safety Reports**: Any reports submitted about safety concerns or misconduct
- **Usage Data**: Information about how you interact with the Platform

### How We Use Your Information
- **Safety and Vetting**: To help maintain a safe community environment
- **Community Management**: To verify membership and manage community activities
- **Reporting**: To track and address safety concerns and misconduct

### Your Rights
You have the right to:
- **Access**: Request a copy of your personal information
- **Correction**: Ask us to correct inaccurate information
- **Deletion**: Request deletion of your information (subject to legal requirements)
- **Portability**: Receive your data in a portable format
- **Objection**: Object to certain uses of your information

## Data Security

### How We Protect Your Information
- **Encryption**: All data is encrypted in transit and at rest
- **Access Controls**: Only authorized community leaders can access your information
- **Audit Logging**: We maintain logs of all access to your information
- **Regular Reviews**: We regularly review and update our security practices

### Data Breach Response
If we become aware of a data breach that affects your information, we will:
- Notify you within 72 hours (or as required by law)
- Provide details about what information was affected
- Explain steps we're taking to address the breach
- Offer assistance to help protect your information
