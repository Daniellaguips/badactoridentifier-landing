# Legal Package Changelog

## Version 2.0 - Terms of Service and Privacy Addendum for Community Leaders
## Version 3.0 - Member Terms of Service and Privacy Policy

### 🚀 Major Improvements

#### Enhanced Legal Protections
- **Indemnification Clause**: Leaders now indemnify the Company against claims arising from their actions
- **Liability Limitation**: Company liability capped at $100 USD
- **Class Action Waiver**: Disputes resolved individually, not through class actions
- **Audit Logging**: All Leader actions are logged for compliance and dispute resolution

#### Strengthened Data Governance
- **Explicit Legal Basis**: Data collection must have proper authorization and legal basis
- **Compliance Requirements**: Explicit mention of GDPR, CCPA, and other applicable laws
- **Member Rights**: Clear requirements for handling member data requests
- **Breach Reporting**: Immediate reporting requirements for suspected breaches

#### Improved Intellectual Property Framework
- **Upload Ownership**: Leaders retain ownership of proprietary content they upload
- **Platform License**: Clear license grant to Company for Platform operation and improvement
- **Data Products**: Permission to generate aggregated, anonymized data products
- **Reverse Engineering**: Explicit prohibition on Platform reverse engineering

#### Enhanced Compliance and Monitoring
- **Immutable Audit Log**: All actions logged for compliance and dispute resolution
- **Monitoring Consent**: Leaders consent to Platform monitoring
- **Cooperation Requirements**: Must cooperate with audits and investigations
- **Immediate Termination**: Company can suspend/terminate immediately for breaches

#### Privacy Addendum v2.0 Improvements
- **Data Controller Clarity**: Clear distinction between Company and Leader data controller roles
- **Data Minimization**: Explicit restrictions on sensitive data categories
- **Confidentiality Obligations**: Clear external disclosure restrictions
- **Cross-Border Processing**: Leaders responsible for cross-border transfer compliance
- **Survival Clause**: Obligations survive termination of Leader status

#### Member Terms of Service v3.0
- **Mr. Number/TrueCaller Shields**: Comprehensive legal protection framework
- **Profile Visibility Consent**: Clear consent for profile linking to reports
- **Liability Release**: Members release Company from report-related claims
- **Section 230 Protection**: U.S. Communications Decency Act protection
- **Arbitration Clause**: Binding arbitration for U.S. users
- **Single Account Policy**: One account per person, duplicate accounts prohibited

#### Member Privacy Policy v1.0
- **Data Collection Clarity**: Clear explanation of what data is collected
- **Profile Visibility**: Transparent about profile visibility to other Members
- **Data Sharing**: Clear policies on data sharing with Leaders and law enforcement
- **Member Rights**: Comprehensive explanation of member rights and how to exercise them
- **Data Retention**: Clear policies on data retention and deletion

### 🔧 Technical Improvements

#### Streamlined Structure
- **Reduced Sections**: From 11 to 11 sections (more focused content)
- **Clearer Language**: More direct and legally precise language
- **Better Organization**: Logical flow from eligibility to termination
- **Consistent Formatting**: Uniform bullet points and numbering

#### Enhanced Cross-References
- **Member Notice**: Clear requirement to provide Member Notice to community members
- **Privacy Addendum**: References to privacy requirements for Leaders
- **Legal Compliance**: Explicit compliance with applicable laws

- **10.2 Class Action Waiver**: Individual dispute resolution only
