The Problem Communities Face

Bad actors exploit the silence between communities, causing repeated harm while leaders struggle to warn each other.

😰

Fear of Blowback

Community leaders, witnesses, and victims are afraid to speak up about problematic behavior due to fear of retaliation, harassment, or legal consequences.

🔄

Community Hopping

Bad actors jump from community to community, exploiting the lack of information sharing between platforms.

🤐

Isolated Suffering

Communities and individuals suffer the same problems in isolation, unable to learn from others' experiences or warn each other about repeat offenders.
