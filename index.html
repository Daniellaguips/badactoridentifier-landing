The Problem

How It Works

Three Steps to a Safer Network

Structured reporting that protects everyone involved.

1

Confidential Report Filed

A member submits a structured safety concern. Reports are confidential by default — but reporters can choose to attach their name to increase credibility if they feel safe doing so.

2

Leaders Review with Context

Verified leaders review reports. Multiple independent reports build a pattern — no single report decides an outcome.

3

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this a background check service?

No. Reports are submitted by community members and are not independently verified by the platform. This tool helps surface patterns — it does not render judgments or replace professional screening.

Who can see my report?

Only verified community leaders with role-based access can view reports. The subject of a report never sees who filed it. All access is logged in an audit trail.

What prevents misuse?

Multiple independent reports are required to surface patterns. Single reports inform but do not determine outcomes. Leaders are accountable through full audit trails, and the platform is open source for public scrutiny.

Is this free during beta?

Yes. All features are free during the beta period. Your feedback directly shapes development priorities and the features we build next.
